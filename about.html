About

Maria Howard

At Maria Howard Portraits, every image is a love letter to family, legacy, and the quiet beauty of togetherness. With a heart shaped by my Peruvian heritage, I bring a deep appreciation for generational bonds and the stories they carry.

Specializing in heartfelt, multi-generational portraits, I blend artistic sensitivity with a warm, respectful approach — capturing the soul in every smile and the magic in everyday moments. Whether you're welcoming a new life, gathering loved ones across generations, or simply celebrating the joy of being together, I create timeless photographs that reflect what matters most: love, connection, and the beauty of family.
