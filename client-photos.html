Your Photos

After your session, I'll email you a private link with your gallery — it's just for you, so please don't share it publicly. Inside, you'll find two folders:

Web / Social — lower-resolution images, perfect for sharing online, texting to family, or setting as a profile photo.

Print / High-Res — full-resolution images, ideal for prints, canvases, or professional printing.

To download, open the link, select the photos you want (or click the folder and choose "Select all"), then click the Download icon. Google Drive will package everything into a zip file for you.

Haven't received your gallery link, or can't find the email? Just reach out below and I'll resend it.
